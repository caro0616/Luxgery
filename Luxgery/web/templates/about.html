About Us

At Luxgery, we specialize in providing a comprehensive aesthetic tourism experience in Medellín, assisting international patients throughout their entire journey — from travel planning to full recovery.

Our commitment is to offer peace of mind, comfort, and full support by taking care of every detail so that each patient can focus solely on their well-being.

What we offer:

  • Flight management and transfer coordination.
  • Surgery scheduling with trusted specialists.
  • Search and booking of suitable recovery houses.
  • Organization of meals and daily transportation.
  • Personalized assistance throughout the entire process.

At Luxgery, we work hand in hand with top specialists in aesthetic procedures and postoperative recovery, ensuring the highest standards of quality and safety at every stage.

Thanks to these strategic partnerships, we are supported by renowned institutions such as our main ally Darien IPS.
